Understanding how characters differ in the apocalypse

Unlike many other MMOs where you strive to improve your (often based on class) primary attributes as much as possible and then your skills, Fallen Earth characters are built within a classless system. This means the approach one takes to building a character is almost reverse that of other more standard modern MMO experiences. A build in Fallen Earth starts with you identifying which abilities/mutations you want to end up with and then working towards those. Fallen Earth builds are focused around a selection of abilities with the intention being to have one of each available type of buff in your build (at least).

Types of buffs available:

  • Aura
  • Stance
  • Toggle
  • Science/Nature/Mutagenics/Medicine
  • Food
  • Drink
  • Tent (construction)

Aura, stance, and toggle buffs are particularly important to be mindful of because in addition to being buffs you cannot purchase from another player, they often effect skills and can be used to save AP in your build.
